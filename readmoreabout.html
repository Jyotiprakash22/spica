Krea Foods and Beverages Limited

The Best For You

Krea Foods and Beverages Private Limited was laid at the Technology Business Incubator, KIIT University, Bhubaneswar, Odisha on the 26 th of August, 2016. As the name suggests, “Krea”, comes from “Creativity” or the urge to work and create new and sustainable technologies. The team thus, focusses towards creating a Health and Wellness (H&W) brand in the Food & Beverage sector using its research expertise in creating a healthy and innovative food and beverage product portfolio primarily for the state of Odisha and the country at large

The long-term objective of the company is to design sustainable technologies to create affordable innovative nutrition dense foods by utilization of local and organic raw material in our state-of-art manufacturing facility. The innovation lies in designing energy-nutrient balance with a ‘no’ or ‘low’ preservative-based strategy. For example, incorporation of locally available goodness of nutrient dense cereals/pulses like jowar, bajra, ragi, soya, oats, gram etc. for products with high shelf-life. The Research and Development (R&D) team focuses to plan affordable and novel baked food formulations using organic components for the demanding market. The motto is about creating healthy foods packed with the natural goodness of indigenous wholesome grains with the target to alleviate malnutrition and supplement nutrition to the masses. This process would contributing to nutritional sustainability along with decreasing the chemical/toxin content in food by the use of biotechnology

Krea aims to fulfill the concept of farm to fork with the idea of developing new innovate, safe and healthy products for the major masses. Our idea is to create a sustainable food structure for the society where nutrition meets food safety and taste.

Krea is the best

Today bakery products have become an essential part of the food basket for a majority population in India. The key factors contributing to popularity of bakery products are increasing urbanization, growing demand for ready to eat convenient product, availability of more options, easy access to such products and affordable pricing. However, there has been a growing concerns of increasing toxicity in foods, rise of use of chemicals, decline of nutritional balance in our daily diet which has caused various health impacts. Krea Foods and Beverages Limited aims to fulfill the untapped demand of healthy bakery products by providing a portfolio of nutritious, organic and hygienic items with the help of R&D innovation in food sciences and food biotechnology. Krea Foods and Beverages Limited has taken lead in establishing a state of the art manufacturing facility housed at Bhubaneswar to cater to the demand of Odisha consumers for healthy and nutritious bakery products through sustainable innovations and R&D.The long-term objective of the company is to club R&D to manufacturing and design sustainable technologies to create affordable innovative nutrition dense foods by utilization of local and organic raw material of the country.

Therefore, the key differentiator of Krea Foods and other unique propositions are as follows:

The innovation lies in designing energy-nutrient balance with a ‘no’ or ‘low’ preservative- based strategy. Use of unique enzyme concoctions using amylases, proteases for processes improvement and to minimize preservatives and synthetic chemical usage like potassium bromate, potassium iodate which are carcinogenic for human consumption.

Incorporation of indigenous goodness of nutrient dense cereals/pulses like millets(mandia), jowar, bajra, ragi, soya, oats, gram etc. for products with high shelf-life in a unique poly-flour system.

Developing of a novel asparaginase based enzyme formulation for decreasing acrylamide, a process toxicant which is carcinogenic and is found in daily foods which consists of 40% of our daily dietary intake.

Greater shelf life of productswhich can be used for consumption during disaster management and for campers, mountaineers and defense personnel.

Providing professional training to tribal students of Odisha in bakery manufacturing, free of cost, under the program Kalinga Khadya Shiksha.

Providing nutritious meals to tribal students of Odisha at a subsidized price as part of contributing back to Odisha society

Sourcing indigenous raw material cereals and pulses directly from rural Odisha thereby creating additional income sources for farmers and tribal citizens.

For its innovation and commercial potential, the product has been supported at an early stage by Development Commissioner -Ministry of Small and Medium Enterprises (DC-MSME) Government of India through KIIT-Technology Business Incubator (KIIT-TBI), KIIT University, Bhubaneswar and further supported with Seed Fund by National Science & Technology Entrepreneurship Development Board (NSTEDB) Government of India. The company has also received the certificate of Startup recognition under Odisha Start up Policy, Government of Odisha. The company is now getting other Private innovation R&D funds to build R&D core and manufacturing unit.
